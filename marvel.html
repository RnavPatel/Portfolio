An intuitive game mechanic made to unite gamers of all skill levels

This game was designed for Marvel’s millennial fans, who often know the brand through its movies but might not be as familiar with gaming. As entertainment shifts from passive experiences like watching movies to hands-on, interactive ones, we wanted to capture the magic of Marvel’s storytelling in a way that anyone could enjoy. By combining team strategy and rich narratives in a simple tabletop format, the game makes the world of Marvel accessible and fun for all skill levels.

Step into your characters and form unlikely teams

Step into the role of your favorite Marvel hero and form legendary alliances never seen in the cinematic universe. Inspired by Marvel Rivals, the game celebrates dynamic team-ups rooted in heroic chemistry and shared lore, letting fans create their dream squads and explore untold stories.

The context of this game is a pop up travelling dome that will go to theatres across the country, bringing gaming to where MCU fans are most comfortable - the theatres. Through a new weekly roster of characters, people can experience new characters every time they play.
