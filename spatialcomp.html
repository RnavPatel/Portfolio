Wander

Using the joy of wandering and discovery to tranlsate journeys from physical maps into a space for a collective experience.

Role: Interaction Designer, Developer

Duration: 5 weeks

Tools: Interaction Design, Spatial Computing, IoT, Unity C#, Arduino

Collaborators : Stanley Ip

Creating a sense of adventure in a space.

How can we translate the joys of worldbuilding on maps and drawings from paper to a space? We can use accurate location data to allow people to create journeys into different worlds within the same room. Users can take drawings and place them within this experience, creating digital destinations and breadcrumbs for “adventurers” in the same room to experience. This is done through spatial computing, as well as a haptic product system that leads viewers into a room of discovery and wandering in this semi-digital space.
